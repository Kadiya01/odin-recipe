Jollof Rice

Description

A vibrant one‑pot rice dish cooked in a rich tomato sauce with peppers, onions, and a blend of spices. It’s a staple at parties and family gatherings across Nigeria.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups parboiled long‑grain rice
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, blended
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, blended
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Protein of choice (chicken, shrimp, or beef) – optional

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions and sauté until translucent.
  2. Add blended red pepper, tomatoes, and tomato paste. Cook for 5‑7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Stir in thyme, curry powder, bay leaf, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add the rice and toss to coat evenly with the sauce.
  5. Pour in the broth, bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20‑25 minutes until rice is cooked and liquid is absorbed.
  6. If using protein, cook it separately and mix in during the last 5 minutes.
  7. Fluff the rice with a fork, garnish with sliced green bell pepper, and serve hot.
