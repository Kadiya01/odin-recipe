Moi Moi

Description

A savory steamed bean pudding made from blended black-eyed peas, peppers, onions, and seasonings. It can be cooked in banana leaves, foil, or a muffin tin and is often enjoyed as a side dish or main course.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups black‑eyed peas (or brown beans), soaked overnight
  • 1 large red bell pepper
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp ground crayfish (optional)
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp palm oil (or vegetable oil)
  • 1 cup water or broth
  • ½ cup diced cooked shrimp, boiled egg, or smoked fish (optional)
  • Banana leaves, foil, or muffin tin for steaming

Steps

  1. Drain the soaked beans and blend them with the bell pepper, onion, garlic, and water until smooth. Scrape the mixture into a large bowl.
  2. Stir in palm oil, crayfish, smoked paprika, cayenne, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Fold in any optional add‑ins (shrimp, egg, smoked fish).
  4. Grease banana leaf pieces, foil squares, or a muffin tin. Spoon the batter into each cup, filling about three‑quarters full.
  5. Steam over medium‑low heat for 45‑60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Allow to cool slightly, then unmold and serve warm with a side of rice, salad, or bread.
